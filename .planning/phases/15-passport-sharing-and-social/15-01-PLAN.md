**Badge card** (`/api/passport/share-card/badge/route.tsx`):
- Query params: `badgeName`, `badgeDesc`, `badgeIcon` (emoji), `rarity`, `category`, `earnedAt`, `fanName`, `slug`
- 1080x1920 layout: DECIBEL header, large emoji icon, badge name, description, rarity pill (common=white, rare=blue, epic=purple, legendary=gold with glow), "Earned by {fanName}", date earned, bottom branding
- Rarity colors: common=#FFFFFF, rare=#4D9AFF, epic=#9B6DFF, legendary=#FFD700

All cards share the same visual language: #0B0B0F background, gradient accent lines top/bottom, DECIBEL header with letter-spacing, fan slug URL at bottom, tagline at very bottom. Follow the existing route.tsx pattern exactly for the satori-compatible JSX (flexbox only, no CSS grid).
